Appointment Form

Please Read This

To make an appointment at Sunrise Care Hospital, please fill out the form below the accurate information. Enter your full name, contact number, and email address so our staff can reach you easily.

Select the department you wish to visit and choose your preferred apointment date and time. Appointments are available from 8:00 AM(morning) to 5:00 PM(evening) , Monday to Friday.

After submitting the form, our team will review your request and contact you within 24 hours* to confirm your appointment. Please note that appointment availability depends on your doctor's schedule.

For urgent cases, we strongly recommend contacting the hospital directly by phone instead of using the appointment form(online).
