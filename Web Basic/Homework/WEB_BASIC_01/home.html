Ⅰ. About Sunrise Care Hospital

Sunrise Care Hospital is a leading healthcare provider in Phnom Penh, offering high-quality medical service since 2012. We focus on patient safety, technology and professional care. Our hospital provides services in cardiogy, pediatrics, surgery, internal medicine an emergency care. With a team of experienced doctors, nurses, and medical staff, Sunrise Care Hospital is committed to delivering accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for every patient. Our specialists work together to ensure the best possible medical outcomes. The hospital is equipped with modern medical equiupment, clean facilities, and comfortable patient rooms to support both outpatient and inpatient services. We continuously improve our services to meet international healthcare standards. At Sunrise Care Hospital, we believe that compassion and respect are essential in healthcare. Our mission is to provide reliable, and patient-centered medical services for individuals, families and the community.

Ⅲ. Department

Here are some of our departments:

  • Cardiology
    •

  • The Cardiology focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related diseases. Cardiologists treat conditions such as heart attacks, irregular heartbeat, and high blood pressure.

    The heart pumps blood at an average of 72 beats per minute. Oxygen is carried by blood cells using O2.

    Modern cardiology uses advanced equipment like ECG® machines.

  • Pediatrics
    •

  • The Pediatrics department provides medical care for infants, children, and teenageers.

    Pediatrians monitor child growth and development and treat common illnesses such as fever, appendix, and infections.

  • General Surgery
    •

  • The General Surgery department performs surgical procedures to treat various diseases and injuries.

    Surgeons operate on organs such as the stomach, appendix, and intestines.

    Patients must fast for at least 8 hours before surgery. Common surgical tools include scapels and forceps.

  • Internal Medicine
    •

  • The Internal Medicine department focuses on preventing, diagnosing and treating adult diseases.

    Internists manage chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension and kidney disease.

    Blood sugar levels are mesured in mg/dL*. A healthy pH level is about 7.4normal.
